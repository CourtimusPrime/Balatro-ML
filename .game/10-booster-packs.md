# Booster Packs

Consumable shop packs that let you draft Tarots, Planets, playing cards, Jokers, or Spectral cards.

> As of: v1.0.1 (base game, ~1.0.1n/1.0.1o)
> Total: 5 pack types x 3 size variants = 15 pack variants

## How packs appear

- **Two pack slots** are offered in every shop (the two slots below the card slots). They are often, but not always, two different pack types.
- Booster Packs do **not** refresh when you reroll the shop (rerolls only re-roll the regular card slots).
- Buying a pack opens it immediately: you are shown a set of cards and **pick** a fixed number; the rest are discarded.
- For consumable packs (Arcana, Celestial, Spectral), you may need an open **consumable slot** to take a card unless it is used immediately on selection. Standard pack cards go into your deck; Buffoon pack Jokers need an open Joker slot.
- The **first shop of a run** offers packs like any other (no special free-pack rule in base v1.0); free packs come from **Tags** (e.g., the various Pack Tags grant a free pack of the corresponding type in the next shop).

### Pattern summary

- **Arcana / Celestial / Standard**: Normal show 3, Jumbo show 5, Mega show 5. Pick 1 / 1 / 2.
- **Buffoon / Spectral**: Normal show 2, Jumbo show 4, Mega show 4. Pick 1 / 1 / 2.
- **Cost is uniform across types**: Normal $4, Jumbo $6, Mega $8.

## Pack contents notes

- **Arcana Pack** — Tarot cards (and, with the **Omen Globe** voucher, Spectral cards may also appear here).
- **Celestial Pack** — Planet cards. With the **Telescope** voucher, the pack always includes the Planet for your most-played poker hand.
- **Standard Pack** — Playing cards that are **added to your deck**. These can come with an **Enhancement** (e.g., Bonus, Mult, Wild, Glass, Steel, Stone, Gold, Lucky), an **Edition** (Foil, Holographic, Polychrome), and/or a **Seal** (Gold, Red, Blue, Purple). The **Hone / Glow Up** vouchers raise edition appearance rates.
- **Buffoon Pack** — Jokers (the only pack type that is purely Jokers). Taking a Joker requires an open Joker slot.
- **Spectral Pack** — Spectral cards (powerful, often with a cost/downside). These packs are rarer in the shop than the others.
